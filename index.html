ABOUT


Researcher | Software & Web Developer | UI/UX Designer | Innovator

From a deep curiosity about how the world works to building solutions that make an impact—I have always believed in the power of innovation, knowledge, and creativity. My journey has taken me across diverse fields, from scientific research and software development to UI/UX design and digital education, all driven by a passion for learning and problem-solving. With a background in Physics, I have explored the world of quantum materials, photonics, and electronics, blending scientific inquiry with cutting-edge technology. As a NASA Space Apps Challenge champion, I developed EarthLink, using NASA’s data to reveal Earth’s interconnected systems—an initiative that continues to inspire public awareness. Technology is more than just code for me; it’s a way to bridge gaps, tell stories, and create meaningful experiences. Through projects like Campus 360, I have crafted digital ecosystems that simplify everyday life, while my work at D’Vincy DevZone and PixelLab Digital has helped shape global design and development projects. Beyond the screen, I have actively contributed to open-source knowledge, enriching Wikipedia and Bengali Wiktionary, and supporting UNICEF and USAID initiatives to leverage technology for education and peace. Every project I take on is fueled by the belief that technology should empower, inform, and inspire change. I am always eager to explore new ideas, collaborate with creative minds, and build solutions that matter. If you're passionate about innovation, let’s connect and create something remarkable together!

NASA Space Apps Challenge 2022: Division Champion

In 2022, I had the privilege of participating in the NASA Space Apps Challenge, where my team emerged as the division champion at our local host. We developed an innovative app designed to alert communities about climate and weather changes, utilizing raw data directly from NASA. My role as the front-end developer allowed me to bring our vision to life through intuitive design and user experience. I also contributed to the presentation, graphical elements, and conducted extensive big data research. Guided by expert mentorship, our collaborative effort demonstrated the power of technology in addressing global challenges.

As part of a university initiative to solve campus challenges, I led my team to victory by developing an innovative app "CAMPUS 360" tailored to the needs of both students and faculty. My role in this project involved both back-end & front-end development, UI/UX design, and overseeing the presentation, ensuring our app stood out for its functionality and user-centric approach. This achievement not only demonstrated our technical skills but also our commitment to improving the campus experience.
